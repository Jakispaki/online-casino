Help & FAQ

Everything you need to know to play Blackjack safely and confidently.

Blackjack basics

  • Goal: get as close to 21 as possible without going over.
  • Number cards count as their value. Face cards count as 10. Aces count as 1 or 11.
  • The dealer draws until they reach 17 or higher.
  • If you go over 21, you bust and lose the hand.

Controls

  • Deal cards: start a new hand with your bet.
  • Hit: take another card.
  • Stand: end your turn and let the dealer play.
  • Roulette: choose a bet type and spin the wheel.
  • Settings: adjust table theme and card size.
  • Add funds: demo top-up for your wallet.

Roulette basics

  • Bet on color (red/black), parity (odd/even), or a single number.
  • Number bets pay 35:1. Color and parity pay 1:1.
  • Zero is green and loses against color/parity bets.

Common questions

  • Why is my card hidden? The dealer keeps one card face down until the hand ends.
  • What is a push? A tie. Your bet is returned.
  • Are deposits real? No. The deposit page is a demo and uses fake card details.
  • Where are my stats? Open the Statistics page from the navigation bar.
  • Forgot password? Contact support and we will reset it for you.

Responsible gaming

Play for fun. Set limits and take breaks. If gambling stops being enjoyable, please seek help.
